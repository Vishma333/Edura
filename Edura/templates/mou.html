Our Global & Industry Collaborations

Building bridges for academic excellence, research innovation, and enhanced student opportunities through strategic partnerships.

Fostering Synergy Through MOUs

Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) are pivotal to Edura's commitment to providing a world-class, globally relevant education. These collaborations with esteemed academic institutions and leading industry players enrich our ecosystem.

Benefits of Our Partnerships

Our MOUs are designed to create a multitude of advantages for our students, faculty, and the wider Edura community:

  • Student Exchange Programs
  • Joint Research Initiatives
  • Faculty Development & Exchange
  • Access to Specialized Resources
  • Internship & Placement Opportunities
  • Industry-Relevant Curriculum Input
  • Dual Degree/Certification Pathways
  • Cultural & Academic Immersion
  • Enhanced Global Networking
  • Workshops & Joint Seminars

Our Esteemed Partners

A look at some of our active Memorandums of Understanding.

TechVerse

TechVerse Solutions International

Type: Industry Partner | Active Since: January 15, 2024

Focus: Collaborative research in AI and Machine Learning, internship programs for Computer Science students, joint workshops on emerging technologies.

GreenLabs

GreenEnergy Labs Worldwide

Type: Research Institute | Active Since: October 3, 2023

Focus: Joint projects on renewable energy solutions, faculty exchange for sustainability studies, access to advanced lab facilities for Electrical and Environmental Engineering students.

Global Uni.

Global University of Innovation

Type: Academic Institution | Active Since: August 20, 2023

HealthTech

HealthcareTech India Consortium

Type: Industry Consortium | Active Since: July 18, 2023

Focus: Innovation workshops for BioTech and Health Informatics students, guest lectures from industry experts, and co-development of healthcare technology modules.
